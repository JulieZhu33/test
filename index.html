Brand Guidelines

This guide defines the visual language, design style, and principles that shape a clear and consistent brand experience, no matter the team or area of expertise.

At its core, Redo is about precision and clarity—just like our mission to correct financial errors and optimize balance sheets. This guide lays out the essential design standards that bring our brand to life, from our color system and typography to accessibility benchmarks and beyond.

01

Brand Strategy

In the world of finance, mistakes happen—miscalculations, overlooked expenses, inefficiencies that silently erode profitability. Businesses lose money not because they aren't earning, but because errors go unchecked.

Redo returns confidence in financial numbers.

Born from the need for financial clarity, Redo was founded on a simple yet powerful mission: to correct financial errors and optimize balance sheets. We believe that precision is the key to profitability, and that businesses shouldn't be held back by auditable losses. With advanced technology and expert analysts, we uncover discrepancies, eliminate inefficiencies, and restore confidence in the numbers that drive success.

At Redo, we don't just fix mistakes—we empower businesses to move forward with accuracy and efficiency. Because when the numbers are right, the future looks even brighter.

Logo

The Redo logo is a symbol, representing a return to normal business operations. The red color symbolizes the need for change and recovery from adversity. This symbol is used in various contexts, including corporate branding and marketing materials.

Redesigning with colors, shapes, lines, and textures can help create a more modern and appealing design. This approach can help businesses stand out from their competitors and attract new customers.

Here are some tips to help you redesign your Redo logo:

  • Use a simple and clean design.
  • Choose a color scheme that reflects your brand personality.
  • Make sure the design is easy to read and understand.
  • Test the design on different devices and platforms.

By following these tips, you can create a Redo logo that is both stylish and effective.

06

Art Direction

Redo's photography style reinforces our brand's core values—trust, clarity, and financial empowerment—by showcasing visuals that reflect professionalism, accuracy, and control.

📷 Modern Workspace

Photography should feature modern, well-lit workspaces with a clean and organized feel. The focus should be on the client—avoid overly dramatic compositions or staged culture shots.

💻 Financial Technology

Include elements of financial technology—computer screens displaying data/records, tables with data analysis, or hands interacting with financial tools—to highlight Redo's tech-driven approach to error correction.

👥 Real Professionals

Images should capture real-world financial compliance professionals analyzing reports, business owners resolving balance sheets, and teams discussing financial strategies. This reinforces Redo's role in helping businesses take control of their finances.
